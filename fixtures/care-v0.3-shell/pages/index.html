Promoting Independence and Dignity in Home Care

At Vigil Care Services, we offer flexible home care assistance that empowers adults in London to live with dignity and choice. Our compassionate team is dedicated to fostering independence through personalised support that truly meets individual needs.

Companionship & Social Support

One-to-one visits that bring warmth, structure, and meaningful social interaction. Whether it's a walk, a shared hobby, or simply a friendly chat, our care support companions offer emotional connection and consistency.

Home Help & Domestic Assistance

We assist with light cleaning, laundry, shopping, and simple meal preparation — making home life easier and more manageable without compromising your autonomy.

Respite for Carers

Caring for a loved one is deeply personal, but everyone needs a break. Our reliable care support team offers short-term sitting and supervision so family carers can step away with peace of mind.

Welfare Visits & Daily Check-Ins

Routine check-ins — in person or by phone — help ensure everything's okay. From wellbeing conversations to hydration prompts, our team offers both reassurance and presence.

Lifestyle Support & Admin Help

We assist with letters, forms, digital reminders, and organising household admin — helping clients stay on top of life and feel in control.

Who We Support

We provide care support to adults aged 18+ who value their independence but need a bit of extra help to thrive.
