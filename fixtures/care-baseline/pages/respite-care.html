Respite Care – Support for Family Carers Across London

Caring for a loved one is rewarding, but it can also be exhausting. At Vigil Care Services, our respite care offers family carers a much-needed break by providing safe, reliable sitting visits for adults who don't require personal or medical care. Whether you need a few hours to recharge, attend an appointment, or simply rest, we're here to support you.

What Our Respite Care Involves

Our respite care is flexible and non-intrusive. We do not provide personal or clinical support. Instead, we offer companionship, presence, and peace of mind while you're away. Sit with your loved one at home Provide light conversation and reassurance Support routine or calming activities (TV, reading, music) Encourage hydration, snacks, or medication prompts Monitor wellbeing and report back any concerns Keep a calm and safe environment

Frequently asked questions

Do you provide personal care during respite visits?
No. Our respite care is for sitting support only. We do not provide any regulated or personal care.
Is this service available evenings or weekends?
Yes, subject to availability. We'll do our best to work around your schedule.
How quickly can we start?
We aim to begin within a few days of your enquiry, especially for families who need short-term relief or regular breaks.
Can the same support worker visit each time?
Where possible, yes. We know familiarity matters — especially for vulnerable adults.
