About Us

Looking to learn more about care support services in London that put people first? At Vigil Care Services, we're not just another support provider — we're a relationship-driven team that helps adults live independently, confidently, and with dignity. Our work is grounded in trust, flexibility, and a deep understanding of everyday challenges faced by individuals and family carers.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To provide flexible, respectful, and relationship-centred support for adults aged 18 and over, enabling them to stay independent and thrive at home — on their own terms. Whether it's a friendly face for conversation, a helping hand with household tasks, or a break for a family carer, we're here to make daily life feel easier, not more complicated.

Get In Touch

What We Do — and What We Don't

Vigil Care Services is different. We are not a care agency, and we do not provide personal or medical care. Instead, we offer: Companionship and emotional support Help with domestic tasks like laundry and shopping Support with routines, paperwork, or admin Sitting visits to give carers a much-needed break Flexible, non-intrusive welfare check-ins This allows us to stay outside of CQC regulation, while still delivering meaningful day-to-day support.
