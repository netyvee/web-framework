Services

When it comes to finding reliable home support options in London, families and individuals often struggle to find services that are both flexible and respectful of independence. At Vigil Care Services, we specialise in providing tailored support for adults aged 18 and over — without the need for personal care or regulated interventions. Our services are designed to fill the gap: practical, human, and built entirely around you.

Companionship & Social Support

Social connection is a basic human need. We provide companionship visits that encourage conversation, shared activities, or simply a reassuring presence. Whether it's enjoying a hobby, going out for fresh air, or staying in for a chat, our companions bring warmth and consistency into everyday life.

Home Help & Domestic Support

Light cleaning and tidying Laundry and ironing Meal preparation (no feeding) Grocery shopping and errand support

Respite for Family Carers

If you're a carer, you know how exhausting continuous support can be. Our respite services allow you to take a break while we spend time with your loved one.

Welfare Checks & Daily Check-Ins

Regular visits or phone calls to check on wellbeing, prompt medication (without administering), and encourage hydration and nutrition.

Lifestyle Support & Administrative Help

Filling in forms Organising mail Scheduling reminders Light digital support (e.g. mobile apps, online appointments)

How Our Support Works

You choose what you need You decide how often we visit We match you with a support worker You stay in control
